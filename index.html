About

Hi, I’m Jack Stoltz – a creative problem solver, aspiring software engineer, and avid explorer of the ever-evolving world of technology.

I’m currently studying Computer Engineering at Georgia Tech, where I’ve had the opportunity to turn bold ideas into impactful projects, from designing a locally-run secure ML model to analyzing sentiments of people's online discourse, and even diving into software-hardware integration with numerous fun Arduino projects.

I thrive on bringing creative applications to life, whether it’s developing tools that make life easier, exploring the cutting edge of generative AI, or collaborating on projects with talented peers. My experiences have shaped not just my technical skills but also my ability to lead, collaborate, and adapt to challenges with curiosity and enthusiasm.

When I’m not deep into code or crafting solutions, you’ll probably find me hiking a scenic trail, shooting hoops on the basketball court, or traveling to discover unique cultures and, of course, their food! Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY, I’ve always been drawn to the energy of big ideas, which inspires everything I create.

If you’re into innovation, great stories, or just need a teammate for your next project, let’s connect! Together, we might just create something extraordinary.
